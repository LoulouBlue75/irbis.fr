# IRBIS — Copywriting v4.0 (Ciselé)

> **Principe** : Maximum impact, minimum mots.
> **Règles** :
> - Headlines ≤ 5 mots
> - Body ≤ 12 mots
> - Si montrable, pas dit
> - Chaque mot pèse

---

## RÈGLES DE RÉDACTION

### Densité
| Élément | Max mots | Exemple |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Tagline | 4 | "High standards. Shared ambition." |
| Headline | 5 | "Decisive appointments." |
| Subhead | 8 | "Executive search for those who won't settle." |
| Card title | 3 | "The Method" |
| Card body | 12 | — |
| CTA | 3 | "Enter →" |

### Ponctuation
- Point final = affirmation forte
- Pas de point = invitation
- → = action, mouvement
- Tiret long (—) = rupture, emphase

### Ce qu'on ne dit JAMAIS
- "We help you..." (condescendant)
- "Our team of experts..." (générique)
- "Best-in-class..." (vide)
- "Leverage..." "Synergy..." (corporate bullshit)
- Superlatifs non prouvés
